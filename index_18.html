This Week On #MediaBuzz

MOLLIE HEMINGWAY, SHELBY HOLLIDAY and MARIE HARF agreed that Brian Ross blundered badly on the Mike Flynn story and deserved his ABC suspension, but they differed on the coverage and speculation surrounding the ex-national security adviser's guilty plea in the Russia probe. LYNN SHERR doubted that no one at NBC knew of Matt Lauer's sexual misconduct, and MATT BELLONI questioned the network's shifting explanations of his firing. SUSAN FERRECHIO said an accuser's "Today" show interview was a factor in turning Democrats against John Conyers but that his backers question why Al Franken isn't facing similar demands to resign. And NOAH ROTHMAN said the Washington Post did a good job exposing a sting involving phony allegations against Roy Moore and said conservative media activists are becoming the kind of partisans they once denounced.

About the Show

Our new media program analyzes the coverage of a wide range of topics, including technology, social media, politics, culture and sports. Our panelists debate the week’s hottest press issues and top journalists join us for in-depth interviews.

About Howard Kurtz

Howard Kurtz currently serves as host of FOX News Channel's "#mediabuzz" (Sundays 11 AM-12 PM ET). Based in Washington, D.C., he joined the network in July 2013 and debuted on "Special Report with Bret Baier".
