    TONIGHT on Fox News Channel: Sean Spicer on 'Hannity,' Conyers' Accusers on 'The Story'

    share this
    email
    by Fox News Insider

    Fox News Channel has a power-packed primetime lineup tonight, including interviews with Sean Spicer, Sen. Mitch McConnell and two of John Conyers' accusers.

    At 7:00pm ET on The Story, Martha MacCallum speaks to two of the women who have accused Rep. John Conyers (D-Mich.) of sexual misconduct.

    Melanie Sloan and Deanna Maher will discuss their accusations against the longtime lawmaker, who they told and why nothing was done until now. 

    Conyers stepped down as ranking member of House Judiciary Committee amid an ethics investigation into sexual harassment and workplace abuse allegations by former staffers. Conyers, who has denied the accusations, is facing growing calls to resign.

    Read Full Article

    Former White House Press Secretary Sean Spicer joins Hannity at 9:00 to discuss tax reform, North Korea and fast-tracking President Donald Trump's agenda.

    And at 10:00, Laura Ingraham has exclusive interviews with Senate Majority Leader Mitch McConnell (R-Ky.) and Sen. Marco Rubio (R-Fla.) to discuss the tax reform push.

    Then at 11:00pm ET, Rep. Ron DeSantis (R-Fla.) joins Shannon Bream to talk about why he believes the U.S. embassy in Israel should move to Jerusalem.

    It's all part of a must-see primetime lineup tonight, beginning at 7:00pm ET, on Fox News Channel.
